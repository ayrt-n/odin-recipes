Chicken Quesadillas

Description

A lunch staple, the turkey sandwich (or club) is an easy to prepare meal which can be made on relatively short notice. Bacon is optional, but highly recommended. Try taking your sandwich game to the next level by turning it into a double-decker sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice as to what bread)
  • 1 tbsp garlic mayo
  • 100g pre-sliced turkey
  • 1 tomato, cut into slices
  • 2 slices of cooked bacon
  • 1 leaf of lettuce
  • Frank's Red Hot (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Put the two slices of bread into a toaster and toast until golden brown
  2. Spread garlic mayo across one (or both) slices of bread
  3. Start by placing lettuce and tomato on to one of the slices of bread (with mayo spread on to it)
  4. Stack turkey on top of tomato and lettuce
  5. Place cooked bacon on top of turkey
  6. Add some Frank's Red Hot if you would like
  7. Place last piece of bread on top of the sandwich and cut into triangles and enjoy!

Chicken Quesadillas

Description

A quick and easy recipe for chicken quesadillas. My go to on a night where you don't feel like doing much work and want a quick and easy meal. Easy to adapt and add additional ingredients if desired!

Ingredients

  • 2 10" tortillas
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 cup of chopped chicken
  • 1/4 cup black beans
  • Frank's Red Hot for dipping (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400F and prepare a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil
  2. Place one tortilla on to the baking sheet and spread 1/2 of cheese evenly across tortilla
  3. Place chopped chicken and black beans evenly around tortilla, on top of cheese
  4. Using the rest of the cheese, spread evenly over the tortilla
  5. Cover with the second tortilla and place into pre-heated oven
  6. Cook for 8 minutes, or until golden brown, slice into triangles and enjoy!
